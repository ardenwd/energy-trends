Data Center Energy Usage

Data Source: The U.S. Data Center Energy Usage Report (Berkeley Lab 2024)

In 2025, the US will experience the fastest expansion of the US electric grid in generations. One driving cause is the increase in the energy usage of data centers. In 2023, data centers accounted for 4.4% of the total US electricity usage and is projected to account for as much as 12.0% by 2028.
